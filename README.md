# 📱 Data Expiry Tracker

Never lose your data again! Track all your data subscriptions in one place and get reminded before they expire.

![Data Tracker](https://img.shields.io/badge/Data-Tracker-green) ![GitHub Pages](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Pages-blue)

## ✨ Features

- 📊 **Track All Data** - Add all your data subscriptions (MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9mobile)
- ⏰ **Expiry Countdown** - See exactly how many days left before expiry
- ⚠️ **Expiring Soon Alerts** - Visual indicators when data is about to expire
- 💰 **Cost Tracking** - See how much you spend on data
- 🔄 **Easy Renew** - One-click renewal updates purchase date
- 📱 **Mobile Friendly** - Works perfectly on your phone
- 💾 **Local Storage** - Your data stays on your device

## 🚀 Live Demo

Visit: **https://dammieoptimus.github.io/data-tracker/**

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. **Add Your Data** - Tap "Add New" and enter:
   - Network provider
   - Phone number
   - Data amount
   - Cost
   - Purchase date
   - Validity (days)

2. **Track Expiry** - See countdown for each plan

3. **Get Reminded** - The app shows expiring data at the top

4. **Renew Easy** - Click "Renew" to update to today's date

## 📱 Supported Networks

- MTN
- Airtel
- Glo
- 9mobile

## 💾 Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). No account needed - your data never leaves your device!
